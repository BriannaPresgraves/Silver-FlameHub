Description

Krovna is a psychological folk horror experience that blends environmental storytelling, ritual-based interactions, and eerie exploration. Players uncover disturbing spaces, piece together clues, and use ritual mechanics to manipulate the world around them as they move deeper into an unsettling mystery.

History

Krovna began as a collaborative game project focused on building a tense, atmospheric experience through world design, interaction design, and narrative systems. The team explored ways to make menus, journals, maps, and ritual interactions feel like part of the story itself rather than separate UI layers.

As development continued, the project evolved toward a stronger folk horror identity, with a focus on unsettling visual language, immersive interaction flows, and a world that feels strange, fragmented, and alive.

Features

  • Narrative-driven psychological folk horror
  • Ritual-based interactions tied directly to exploration
  • Immersive journal, inventory, and map systems
  • Atmospheric environments with unsettling visual storytelling
  • Puzzle and traversal mechanics that reshape space
