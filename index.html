Krovna

A psychological horror puzzle RPG set within a sentient house rooted in Slavic folklore. Trapped in a shifting, deceptive environemnt, players explore to uncover family secrets and perform rituals to survice the corrupted spirit. Each action carries a physical or mental cost, forcing players to balance sacrifice, strategy, and survival as they attempt to escape.
