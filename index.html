Krovna

A psychological horror puzzle RPG set within a sentient house rooted in Slavic folklore. Trapped in a shifting, deceptive environemnt, players explore to uncover family secrets and perform spells to survive the corrupted spirit.

Features

Spell-Based Interactions

Use three spells—Switch, Rotate, and Flash—from your spell book to manipulate the environment, solve puzzles, and reveal what the house is hiding.

Escalating Environmental Puzzles

Challenges build over time, starting with learning each spell and eventually combining them under pressure, with puzzles built directly into the space through mismatched doors, rotating rooms, and hidden or fake objects.

Folk Horror Atmosphere

Inspired by Slavic domestic imagery, the world is filled with details like samovars, icons, dried herbs, armchairs, and feather pens to create a grounded but unsettling tone.
